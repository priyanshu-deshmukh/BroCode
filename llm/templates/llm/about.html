About Our Company

Social Pulse is a real-time sentiment analysis platform that tracks public opinion on Twitter and Reddit. It classifies content as Positive, Neutral, or Negative, extracts trending topics using TF-IDF, and presents insights through interactive charts and graphs. Designed for businesses, researchers, and content creators, Social Pulse helps understand audience sentiment and stay ahead of emerging trends.
